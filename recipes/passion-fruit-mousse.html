Passion fruit mousse

Time for a refresher: you'll learn how to prepare a delicious passion fruit mousse!

For those who are not familiar with passion fruit, it is a refreshing tropical fruit, pretty popular in south american countries.

It has an citrus-like flavor when fresh, but it becomes sweeter as it ripens.

Well, let's get to the recipe then.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons unflavored gelatin
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1 ⅓ cups cold heavy cream,
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 cup passion fruit pulp
  • ⅓ cup fresh passion fruit pulp with seeds
  • ¼ cup sugar

Steps

  1. Combine the fresh passion fruit pulp and the sugar in a saucepan
  2. Cook over medium low heat, until the sugar dissolves and the mixture starts boiling
  3. Lower the heat and continue cooking until it thickens slightly
  4. Let the it cool to room temperature
  5. Mix the gelatin and water in a small bowl, until the gelatin is hydrated
  6. Microwave for 30 seconds for the gelatin to melt and let it cool slightly
  7. Combine the heavy cream, sweetened condensed milk, passion fruit pulp and melted gelatin in the blender
  8. Blend for 5 minutes
  9. Transfer the mousse to a large serving bowl
  10. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours to set
  11. Top with the passion fruit sauce
